Welcome

Welcome to my online home. I'm a technologist with over twenty years of information and communications experience in the nonprofit sector. Through my business
S C M Guru LLC, I produce creative technology solutions and entertainment.

I contribute to free and open source software and hardware by writing documentation, producing videos, submitting bug reports and fixes, taking on maintainership responsibility of orphaned packages, and sharing my own freely licensed projects. On the entertainment side of the business, I live stream my music creations and video game play.

If you value free and open source software and hardware, or find my work useful or entertaining, please consider becoming a member. It not only provides me with needed support but helps others too.

Thank you for visiting and stay safe. It's a dangerous business, going out of your door.
