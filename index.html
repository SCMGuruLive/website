Welcome

Welcome to the home of Scott MacCallum and my business S C M Guru LLC. I'm a technologist with over twenty years of information and communications technology experience in the nonprofit sector. Through S C M Guru LLC I contribute to free and open source software by writing documentation, producing videos, submitting bug reports/fixes, and developing projects of my own which I make freely available.

Due to a late diagnosis of autism, and my chosen path of people over profit, I'm not independently wealthy. If you too value free and open source software, or if you find my work useful to you, please consider becoming a member. It really does help and it is appreciated.
